About Us

Welcome to Draggin' Weights! We are so happy to see you are interested in becoming the best you possible! We offer a wide variety of resources from free weights and weightlifting, to weekly competetions every Friday night. We also boast a vending machine of healthy snacks and rejuvenating drinks.

Why are we called Draggin' Weights? Well, our founder, Jules, is a cross dresser who wants to keep in the best shape they can. As a drag queen, they have gone through some rough times, people disparraging against them. Draggin' Weights is a way for them to flaunt all they've got and continue to spread the love of working out to others in the communiy. This gym is their heart and soul, and has even captured the eyes of Andrea Rhodea, re-knowned owner of the HoneyBee Inn. As Andrea says often, "Beauty isn't limited to a gender", and in that spirit, Draggin' Weights is open to ALL: man, woman or anything else!

You'll notice a lot of gyms make a point to have a 'non-competition' environment. That is not the case here. This is a place designed to push you to your limits to achieve the highest heights. How can you get there if you never work with anyone? Or against anyone? For that reason, EVERY Friday night, at 6 pm local, we off a Friendly competition. We do squats, pull ups and push ups. Walk ins are always welcome! What is the reward? The crying of muscles that have been worked to the bone! If you aren't sore tomorrow, you haven't worked hard enough; that's our philosphy!

Jules is our daytime trainer. He has been training folks for years and loves to pass on his knowledge and dedication to every new soul that wanders in. He works closely with Andrea Rhodea and has been the lifeblood behind the competetions we hold for years. If you feel up to it, challenge him!
Sephiroth is our combat trainer. He will make life miserable for you for a long while, but you'll realize that it's all for a reason and you'll come back stronger each time. He mostly works in the evenings, but if you ask, he usually will make time for a quick battle during the day. Please schedule an appointment first!

Hard work pays off!
